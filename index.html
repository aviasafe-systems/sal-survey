This Safety Culture Survey is designed to assess the safety climate within your organization. Your honest responses will help identify strengths and areas for improvement in our safety management system. This survey implements the principles of:

This assessment tool is designed to support the Safety Culture component of ICAO Annex 19 (Safety Management) and follows the guidance framework outlined in ICAO Doc 9859 (Safety Management Manual), 4th Edition. The survey structure addresses:

  • SMS Component 1: Safety Policy and Objectives
  • SMS Component 2: Safety Risk Management
  • SMS Component 3: Safety Assurance
  • SMS Component 4: Safety Promotion
